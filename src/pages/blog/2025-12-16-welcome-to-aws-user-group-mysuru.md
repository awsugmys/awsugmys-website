# Welcome to AWS User Group Mysuru!

We're thrilled to announce the launch of AWS User Group Mysuru - a community dedicated to bringing together AWS enthusiasts, developers, solution architects, and cloud professionals in the beautiful city of Mysuru.

## Our Mission

AWS User Group Mysuru aims to:

- **Learn**: Share knowledge about AWS services, best practices, and emerging technologies
- **Network**: Connect with like-minded professionals and build lasting relationships
- **Grow**: Help each other advance in our cloud journey through mentorship and collaboration

## What to Expect

Our meetups will feature:

- Technical talks by industry experts
- Hands-on workshops and demos
- Networking opportunities
- Q&A sessions with AWS professionals
- Community-driven discussions

## Get Involved

Whether you're just starting with AWS or you're a seasoned cloud architect, there's a place for you in our community. Here's how you can get involved:

1. **Join our Meetup group** - Stay updated on upcoming events
2. **Follow us on social media** - Get the latest news and updates
3. **Volunteer to speak** - Share your knowledge with the community
4. **Sponsor an event** - Help us grow the community

## Upcoming Events

Stay tuned for our first meetup announcement! We're planning an exciting launch event that will set the tone for our community.

## Contact Us

Have questions or want to get involved? Reach out to us at info@awsugmys.in
